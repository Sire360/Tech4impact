Who We Are


Tech for Impact is a youth-driven initiative that harnesses the power of technology to solve real-world problems in communities.


From building digital tools that promote education access to creating platforms that address climate change, we focus on impactful innovation. We believe in collaborative growth, inclusive tech, and purpose-driven design. Our team consists of developers, climate advocates, educators, and change-makers committed to sustainable development.
